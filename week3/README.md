# Week 3: Interactive Tutorials

**Theme:** Build something using AI that teaches humans (or other AIs) about a topic—interactive, engaging, and baked into fun experiences.

> *"Real learning sticks when it's effortless."* Think video game tutorials, memory palaces, and learning that feels like play.

## Ideas to explore

- **AI-Powered Adventure Game Tutorial** — Text or graphical quests where players learn history or coding; AI generates scenarios and feedback.
- **Interactive Quiz World** — Virtual map (e.g. geography, science); AI quizzes at landmarks, rewards for right answers.
- **Gamified Language Learner** — AI chatbot for role-playing in a new language; level up skills like gaming.
- **AI Simulation for Skills** — Virtual labs (chemistry) or driving games (physics); tutorials embedded in gameplay.
- **Personalized Story Builder** — AI tool for stories/comics on math or history; teaches while you create.
- **Memory Palace Game** — AI-built virtual palaces where users place facts as items; navigate to recall (Method of Loci).
- **Collaborative AI Tutor Game** — Multiplayer challenges with AI guiding group learning.

## Spotlight: Memory Palace + AI

The **Method of Loci** uses familiar places to store info—your brain is great at spatial recall. Use AI to generate custom palaces, quiz as you explore, and turn formulas/code into memorable “locations” and stories.

**Enhanced direction (Week 3 build):** Audio quiz questions and feedback (TTS); optional upload of photos of your room/house so the palace is *your* space. AI suggests loci from your images and generates vivid associations for anything you want to remember.
